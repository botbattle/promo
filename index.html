What is Bot!Battle!?

The Bot!Battle! system is a web-based application that facilitates computer science education through programming challenges. To complete these challenges, users write simple programs that control a player's action in an animated game.

The goal of Bot!Battle! is to create a fun and educating environment which will help bring a level of excitement to students. Through this we hope to foster a growth in love for Computer Science, and to help educate and guide students into the wonderful field of Computer Science.

The Bot!Battle! system was originally thought of by Dr. Jeremy Blum of Penn State University. The original design was implemented by three teams of Computer Science students from Penn State Harrisburg, each focusing on different aspects of the project. Our team focused on the Bot!Battle! web application.
